The Design Process

How we turn your imagination into reality.

1. The Discovery Call

A 30-minute virtual chat to discuss your date, venue, budget, and overall vibe. We'll review your Pinterest boards and assess the scale of the project.

2. Moodboarding & Colors

We curate a custom digital moodboard featuring exact Pantone color matching, texture selections (matte, chrome), and floral integrations.

3. Site Survey & Rendering

For large-scale setups, we visit the venue to map out structural rigging points and provide a digital rendering of the final architecture.
