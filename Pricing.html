How We Quote

Every installation is a custom architectural piece. Our quotes are based on three fundamental elements: Volume, Complexity, and Logistics. We pride ourselves on transparent, itemized pricing so you know exactly where your investment goes.

  • Volume (Length & Density) Prices typically start at $25 per linear foot for standard organic garlands. Thicker, luxurious "double-stuffed" garlands increase the base rate.
  • Complexity & Add-Ons Integration of florals, neon signage, custom vinyl decals, and foil clusters add to the overall design fee.

Logistics & Fees

Delivery

Calculated at $2.50 per mile from our studio to your venue. Minimum $40 delivery fee applies to all orders.

Installation

Installation fees are 20% of the total design cost. This covers on-site rigging, structural integrity, and polishing.

Strike (Teardown)

Optional strike service starts at $150. Mandatory for rentals (backdrops, neons, frames) ensuring safe removal.

Pricing FAQ

Why are custom balloons so expensive?

You are paying for high-quality, 100% biodegradable latex that won't pop easily, double-stuffing labor for custom colors.

Is a deposit required?

Yes, a 50% non-refundable retainer is required to secure your date on our calendar. The remaining balance is due 7 days prior to your event.

Do I get to keep the balloons?

Absolutely! The balloons are yours to keep. However, any structural frames, backdrops, and neon signs are rentals.

Do you have an order minimum?

Yes, our minimum for on-site installation is $450 (excluding delivery). For grab-and-go garlands, there is no minimum.
